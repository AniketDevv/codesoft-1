Why E-learning?

Accessibility

E-learning allows students to access course materials and participate in classes from any location and at any time. This is particularly beneficial for those with busy schedules or those who live in remote areas.

Cost-Effectiveness

E-learning can be more affordable than traditional classroom education. It eliminates the need for physical materials, travel expenses, and sometimes even accommodation.

Flexibility

Learners can progress through materials at their own pace, taking more time on challenging topics and moving quickly through areas they understand well.
